About Bliss Body Butter

Our Bliss Body Butter is a luxurious treat for your skin. Made with the finest natural ingredients sourced responsibly, this body butter provides intense hydration while nourishing your skin from within.

The rich, creamy texture glides effortlessly onto your skin, absorbing quickly without leaving a greasy residue. Perfect for daily use, it helps restore your skin's natural moisture barrier and leaves you feeling pampered and refreshed.

Whether you have dry, sensitive, or normal skin, Bliss Body Butter adapts to your needs, providing customized care that keeps your skin healthy, supple, and radiant all day long.

Key Ingredients

  • Shea Butter: Rich in vitamins A and E, provides deep moisturization and helps improve skin elasticity
  • Coconut Oil: Natural antibacterial properties, deeply penetrates to nourish and protect
  • Vitamin E: Powerful antioxidant that helps protect skin from environmental damage
  • Sweet Almond Oil: Lightweight and easily absorbed, helps soften and smooth skin texture
  • Cocoa Butter: Forms a protective barrier to retain moisture and improve skin tone
  • Essential Oils: Natural fragrance blend of lavender and vanilla for a calming, luxurious scent

All ingredients are 100% natural, cruelty-free, and sustainably sourced. Free from parabens, sulfates, and artificial fragrances.

How to Use

For best results, apply Bliss Body Butter to clean, slightly damp skin immediately after bathing or showering. This helps lock in moisture and enhances absorption.

Step-by-step application:

  • Take a generous amount of body butter and warm it between your palms
  • Apply in gentle, circular motions, starting from your feet and working upward
  • Pay extra attention to dry areas like elbows, knees, and heels
  • Allow a minute or two for complete absorption before dressing
  • Use daily for best results, or as needed throughout the day

Pro tip: For an extra luxurious experience, apply before bed and let the butter work its magic overnight. Wake up to softer, smoother, deeply nourished skin.
